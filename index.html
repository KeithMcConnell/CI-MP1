Welcome to the Mesozoic Era: The Age of Dinosaurs The Mesozoic Era, often referred to as the "Age of Reptiles," was a period of dramatic geological and biological change that spanned roughly 252 to 66 million years ago. It's a time that captured the imagination of countless people, thanks in large part to the iconic dinosaurs that roamed the Earth during this era.
